About Me

Greetings! My name is Connor,


I am a passionate software engineer with a thirst for knowledge and a commitment to excellence.

Currently, I am in the final stages of completing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, specializing in software engineering. My journey into the world of technology has been anything but conventional. After graduating from high school, I embarked on a soul-searching adventure along the picturesque California coast. Embracing a minimalist lifestyle, I converted a school bus, which I purchased by selling sheep at the county fair during my high school years, into my humble home. Along the way, I explored various odd jobs and discovered my passion for photography, capturing breathtaking images of rock climbing and surf products for prominent outdoor companies. However, my true calling lay in the realm of software engineering. Fuelled by a deep desire for technical expertise, I embarked on a self-taught journey to learn programming as a hobby. I thrive on the challenge of mastering complex subjects from scratch and consider myself a "professional beginner" - someone who fearlessly dives into new and difficult concepts. Through countless hours of dedicated learning, I have acquired a wide range of technical skills, always striving for growth and improvement. But it's not just about technical prowess for me; it's about using those skills to make a real impact. I am excited to transition into a professional and collaborative environment where I can contribute to meaningful projects and continue my journey of personal and professional development. Aside from my technical expertise, I possess strong problem-solving abilities, meticulous attention to detail, and excellent communication skills. I thrive in teamwork, cherishing the opportunity to collaborate with talented individuals who share a similar drive for success. As you navigate through my website, I hope you gain a glimpse into my world as a software engineer and the passion that fuels my work. I am always seeking new challenges and opportunities to contribute to innovative projects. I would be thrilled to connect with like-minded individuals and organizations who share my enthusiasm for pushing the boundaries of technology. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out if you'd like to connect or discuss potential collaborations.

Best regards,
Connor
